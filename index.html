Background

Vajra Drone Club is a student-driven initiative aimed at fostering innovation and hands-on learning in the field of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and drone technology. The club provides a platform for students to collaborate on designing, building, and flying drones for various applications such as aerial photography, surveillance, and research. Members of the Vajra Drone Club engage in projects that explore cutting-edge drone technologies, participate in national competitions, and work on real-world challenges, promoting a spirit of teamwork, creativity, and technical expertise.
